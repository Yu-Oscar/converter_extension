How to Use

1. Select time, temperature, or length text on any webpage (e.g., "7:30 PM ET", "25°C", "5 feet")
2. Right-click and choose "Convert selection"
3. View results in a smart tooltip that appears near your selection
4. For times: Use the interactive editor to adjust hours, minutes, AM/PM, and timezone
5. See real-time conversion to your configured primary and secondary units

Supported Formats

Time Formats:

  • 12-hour format: 7:30 PM, 2:15 am, 8pm, 3:45 AM
  • 24-hour format: 18:30, 14:15, 23:59, 08:00
  • Military time: 0800, 1430, 2359, 0030
  • With timezones: 7:30 PM ET, 18:30 CET, 14:00 JST, 3:00 PM EST, 9:45 AM PDT, 0800 EST
  • UTC offsets: 18:00 UTC+5, 8pm GMT-8

Temperature Formats:

  • Celsius: 25°C, 25 °C, 25c, -10°C, 25 celsius
  • Fahrenheit: 77°F, 77 °F, 77f, -4°F, 77 fahrenheit
  • Degree symbol only: 160°, 25.5°, -10° (shows both C→F and F→C conversions)

Length Formats:

  • Metric: 5m, 25cm, 10mm, 2km, 50μm (metres, centimetres, millimetres, kilometres, micrometres)
  • Imperial: 12in, 6ft, 3yd, 5mi (inches, feet, yards, miles)
  • Full words: 5 metres, 25 centimeters, 12 inches, 6 feet
  • Symbols: 12", 6' (inches and feet shorthand)

Supported Timezones (65 total):
Popular: ET, PT, GMT, CET, IST, HKT, JST, KST, BRT, AEST
North America: CT, MT, AT, NT, MXT, EST, EDT, CST, CDT, MST, MDT, PST, PDT
Europe/Middle East: EET, MSK, WET, TRT, IRST, IST_IL, UA
Asia: CST_CN, GST, PKT, ICT, SGT, MYT, PHT, WIB, WITA, WIT
Oceania: AWST, NZST, HST, FJT, PGT
South America: ART, COT, PET, CLT, VET
Africa: CAT, WAT, EAT, EET_EG, WET_MA

Smart Features

Multi-Unit Support: Automatically detects and converts time, temperature, and length values
Interactive Time Editor: Adjust detected times using number inputs and dropdowns for precise control
Smart Conflict Resolution: CST defaults to US Central Time in text parsing, but China CST is available via manual selection
Popular Zone Priority: Most-used timezones appear first in dropdowns for quick access
Automatic DST Handling: Built-in daylight saving time adjustments using IANA timezone data
Simple Temperature Conversion: Automatic Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit conversion with configurable precision
Comprehensive Length Conversion: Supports 9 length units with smart precision formatting
Real-time Conversion: Live updates as you modify time values without manual refresh
Global Coverage: Support for 57 timezone abbreviations covering major population centers worldwide
